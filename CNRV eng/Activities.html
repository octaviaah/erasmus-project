Activities

National College "Roman-Voda" takes part in a lot of local projects, also national projects, and it organises multiple internal projects, which aim at the personal development of our students. These projects offer a lot of benefits for both students and teachers, because they aim to involve the students, to reduce absenteeism, to stimulate the development of cooperative learning abilities and to rise the scholar performances. The students are becoming, in this way, ambassadors for a cleaner planet, health and sports promoters, who must make essential changes in this society. The teachers are becoming mentors and are engaging affectionately alongside the students, relieving, at the same time, their childhoos and teenage years.
