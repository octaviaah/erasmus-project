Lazarou CTC

Lazarou CTC is one of the leading IT services, consulting and support providers in the local area, with offices in Mandria and Paphos. The company offers a wide variety of solutions to customers ranging from home users and students to companies and industries. Their constant interest in self-development and rich experience in the field empowered the Lazarou staff to offer our customers a wide array of systems and services, with the aim to maximize employee productivity, deliver security, achieve cost-effectiveness and increase employees’ efficiency. Lazarou CTC’s mission is “to deliver the best quality services and support based on the latest technological advances.” “Quality service” translates into offering quality guarantees and customer support for any products and service purchased from our company along with treating each and every customer as a unique person/company. Therefore, the company focuses on developing tailored hardware and software solutions.

Lazarou CTC operates an information and communication centre focused on both hardware and software development and training. Lazarou CTC specialises in providing individuals and organizations with the technical skills necessary to succeed professionally. We offer a wide range of technical training for Microsoft© , CISCO©, and Adobe©, designed to increase productivity for the end users. For people and students interested in learning new office software skills, or improving their current skills and efficiency, we offer a wide variety of Microsoft Office XP and 2007 courses and training placements to benefit both beginner and advanced users preparing them for ECDL Exams.

Lazarou CTC is dedicated to helping people solve their computer problems and networking issues; while providing ICT education on the technologies they interact with daily.
